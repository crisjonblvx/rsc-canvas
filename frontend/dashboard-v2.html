Accessibility Check

Help build something that's never existed before.

Most AI systems learned about educators like you from the outside. Bonita is being built differently.

heyBonita.AI is a culturally fluent AI assistant being constructed from the inside out — from the voices, the course materials, the assignments, and the teaching philosophy of Black and brown educators who have always known how to reach their students.

If you choose to contribute, your anonymized course content — the assignments you create, the discussions you build, the quizzes you design — becomes part of Bonita's foundation. Not scraped. Not borrowed. Built by people like you, for students like yours.

Your personal information is never shared. Your content is fully anonymized before it is used. You can withdraw your contribution at any time.

This is optional. But if you choose to participate, you're not just saving time on your own courses. You're helping build something your students' students will benefit from.
